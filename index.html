Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Convocation 2025 | 2–4:30 p.m., Jan. 16

Every year, CU Boulder holds a free event to commemorate the Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., one of the country’s most influential civil rights leaders. This year’s convocation features keynote speaker Isabel Wilkerson, Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist and bestselling author of Caste and The Warmth of Other Suns. Learn about parking, seating and other details for registered attendees.
